Welcome

We're having a party! May 20-22, 2016 at the Marin Headlands Hostel.

Accomodation

We have reserved the entire main house of the Marin Headlands Hostel for the weekend, and we would love to have you stay there as our guests. It is walking distance to the ocean, and where we will be hosting meals and other merriment.

Transportation

_By Bike._ The Marin Headlands Hostel is a beautiful bike ride (~12 miles) from San Francisco or a gorgeous ferry trip (30 min) plus bike ride from Sausalito (~5 miles).

_By Bus._ The Muni 76 bus comes to the Headlands from San Francisco only on Saturdays and Sundays.

_By Taxi._ If you're coming straight from the airport, check out Marin Door to Door.

_By Car._ It's about a 25 minute drive from downtown San Francisco. We encourage you to take the scenic route via Conzelman Road.

Activities

Stay tuned for more details about hiking, swimming, dancing, eating, drinking and enjoying all that the location has to offer!
